Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

StatusVault is developed by Aurelion Labs. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how StatusVault handles your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

StatusVault does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, messages, or WhatsApp chats.

The app only accesses WhatsApp Status media stored on your device after you grant permission.

Storage Permission

StatusVault requires storage access solely to display and save WhatsApp Status images and videos selected by you.

  • View WhatsApp Statuses
  • Save Statuses locally
  • Manage saved files

Your files never leave your device unless you choose to share them.

Advertising

StatusVault uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect anonymous information such as advertising identifiers and device information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

For more information please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics

StatusVault does not collect analytics data itself. If Firebase Analytics is enabled in future versions, only anonymous usage statistics may be collected to improve app performance.

Data Storage

All saved images and videos remain stored locally on your device.

StatusVault does not upload your files to any server.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services

Children's Privacy

StatusVault is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
